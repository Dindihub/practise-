Akan Name Generator

This web application takes your birthday details and calculates the day of the week you were born depending on your gender, gives you your Akan Name. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture where children are given their first name as a 'day names' which corresponds with the day ofthe week they were born.

Generate your own Akan name by filling in your details and clicking submit
